More Than Just a Snack: A Story of Becoming

Welcome to the Nutball story. I’m Oseyili Joan Enaboifoh, and I’m so glad you’re here. If you’ve ever felt like you were standing in the shadows, waiting for your moment to step into the light, please know—you are not alone.

From the Dark to the Dawn

For a long time, I lived in fear. I felt the weight of expectations I couldn’t meet and the sting of judgments that said I wouldn't amount to anything. In those quiet, dark places, it’s easy to believe the lies that say you are limited.

Then, I found Jesus. I found life, and in that encounter, I discovered that my potential wasn't defined by people’s opinions, but by God’s design. I realized that the things I thought were "broken" in me were actually the foundation for something new. I stopped listening to the limitations and started listening to the call to grow.

The Nutball Craft

Nutball was born out of that breakthrough. It is my way of taking the meticulous, restorative energy I found in faith and pouring it into something tangible.

Whether I’m formulating the perfect crunch in my botanical recipes or solving a complex logic problem as a software development student, my process is the same: Intentionality. I believe that what we put into our bodies and what we put into our minds should be done with care, purpose, and excellence.

"I am breaking the ramifications of a past that said I was small. Every jar of Nutball is a celebration of that freedom—and I invite you to join me in that celebration."

This business is my testimony that you are more than what people say you are. When you enjoy a Nutball product, I hope you taste more than just ingredients—I hope you taste the courage it took to create them. Let’s continue to break limitations, one step (and one snack) at a time.
